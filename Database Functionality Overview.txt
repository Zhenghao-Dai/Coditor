DATABASE FUNCTIONALITY

SQL Tables:
1) 'Document'
2) 'UserAccount'
3) 'DocumentMaster'
4) 'AccountMaster'

'DocumentMaster' and 'AccountMaster' will be the two master lists for keeping track of which users have access to which documents. Each document will have a limit of 10 users allowed per document.
This does not account for guest users, since they will not be registered users. Each user will have a limit of 10 documents that they can be the creator (host) of. Users can have access to more 
than 10 documents, but this access will only be reflected in the 'DocumentMaster' database.

DocumentMaster Format:
Document | User1 | User2 | User3 | User4 | ... | User10 |

AccountMaster Format:
User1 | Document1 | Document2 | Document3 | Document4 | Document5 | ... | Document10 |

Functions:
1) addUser(params)
2) removeUser()
3) authenticateUser()
4) addDocument()
5) deleteDocument()
